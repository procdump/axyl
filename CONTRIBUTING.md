## Contributing

Rayls Network is **not accepting external contributions at this time.** The project is
under active development by the core team.

We expect to open to community contributions in the future. When we do, a Contributor
License Agreement (CLA) will be required and full contribution guidelines will be
published here.

In the meantime, you're welcome to **open an issue** to report a bug or suggest an
improvement.
